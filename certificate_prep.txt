Use selfback_idi_ntnu_no.jks, turn it into p12 with this

keytool -importkeystore -srckeystore selfback_idi_ntnu_no.jks -destkeystore smartwork_cert.p12 -deststoretype pkcs12 -srcstoretype jks  -storepass pswd -srcstorepass pswd
then use this to get key and certs separately

openssl pkcs12 -in smartwork_cert.p12 -nocerts -out smartwork_cert.key
openssl pkcs12 -in smartwork_cert.p12 -nokeys -out smartwork_cert.pem
